  Shirley Vaughan was born in Gary, Indiana the year the 'Peace Sign' was created as the logo in the struggle against nuclear weapons. She studied Pollution Treatment Technology and Communication in college with the hope of making the world a better place. Her two novels called 'Heaven's Plan' and 'The Will of God' offer insight into embracing peace and world preservation.
